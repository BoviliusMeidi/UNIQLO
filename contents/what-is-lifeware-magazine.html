What is LifeWear Magazine

LifeWear is clothing designed to make everyone's life better. Simple, high-quality essential clothing with a sense of beauty and craftsmanship in every detail. Designed and continuously developed to meet the needs of everyone's life.

As a way of helping people around the world to explore the specifications of these garments and the concepts behind their production, we established LifeWear magazine.

Artwork by Hanna Peterson

On June 2, 1984, the first store opened in Fukuromachi, Hiroshima Prefecture. At that time, the store was still called UNIQUE CLOTHING WAREHOUSE. Although the store name has changed now, our mission to create essential clothing that everyone can wear remains the same. Meanwhile, these classic items have gradually evolved, bringing better functionality with the latest materials to produce lighter garments and softer materials. We aim for these garments to become favorite items that you will wear for years. For this excellence to continue, it is important to constantly evaluate the entire manufacturing process.

In this issue, we think about how LifeWear essentials can be combined to create new fashion colors. For inspiration, we look back to the city and the life that thrives in it. The “mix-and-match” seen in Edinburgh, a historic city where charming people and students from all over the world gather. A weekend in Stockholm, where modern tonal colors are the standard. Or perhaps the refreshing casual wear of today's Paris. Each city has its own characteristics, as well as its new colors.

Timeless clothing is clothing that can be worn at any time. LifeWear continues to develop with this principle in mind...

The illustrations for this “Timeless Tone” cover and tutorial are by Hanna Peterson, an artist based in Sweden. A former pattern designer, her work shows a detailed exploration of textiles and has an impressively soft, natural touch.

Magazine B x UNIQLO Special Issue!

B Magazine is a documentary magazine from South Korea that introduces one brand in each issue, and selects “well-balanced brands” from around the world. Celebrating our fortieth anniversary, they dedicated an entire issue to UNIQLO. This special issue includes a history of the company, a visit to our office, quotes from artists about their views on UNIQLO, and opinions on LifeWear magazine.

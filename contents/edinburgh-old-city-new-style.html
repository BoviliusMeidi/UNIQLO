Living in Edinburgh quickly teaches you about how weather is an aspect of life in the city, and as a result, we have such a diverse wardrobe, that UNIQLO's arrival in Edinburgh was met with great excitement and relief. Summer days usually start with a cloud of fog - or as we call it “The Haar” - coming in from the Firth of Forth. It's not unusual to see commuters wearing winter jackets in August. But the funny thing about Edinburgh's weather is that, just as everyone is getting to work, The Haar disappears. As the cold clouds evaporate, the temperature can rise up to 10 degrees in a matter of minutes. To cope with this dramatic situation, locals would put on thick layers of clothing and then take them off one by one. Thus, there are clothes everywhere, in homes, offices and public spaces where we spend time. The result of this irresponsible habit is like endless karma, we are stuck in a cycle of borrowing and returning each other's clothes. Well, as long as the sizes match...

LifeWear is clothing designed to make everyone's life better. Simple, high-quality essential clothing with a sense of beauty and craftsmanship in every detail. Designed and continuously developed to meet the needs of everyone's life.

As a way of helping people around the world to explore the specifications of these garments and the concepts behind their production, we established LifeWear magazine.

Artwork by Hanna Peterson

On June 2, 1984, the first store opened in Fukuromachi, Hiroshima Prefecture. At that time, the store was still called UNIQUE CLOTHING WAREHOUSE. Although the store name has changed now, our mission to create essential clothing that everyone can wear remains the same. Meanwhile, these classic items have gradually evolved, bringing better functionality with the latest materials to produce lighter garments and softer materials. We aim for these garments to become favorite items that you will wear for years. For this excellence to continue, it is important to constantly evaluate the entire manufacturing process.
